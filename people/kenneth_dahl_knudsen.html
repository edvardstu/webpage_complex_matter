Research interests

The main activity is on the behaviour of various types of soft matter (such as nanoparticles in suspension, polymers, or amphiphiles), and porous nanostructured materials using a variety of experimental methods. These include small-angle neutron scattering (SANS) and synchrotron-based x-ray scattering. Instrumentation and methodological development of scattering techniques is also of interest.
