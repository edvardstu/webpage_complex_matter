Bi-annual Geilo PhD School

The group is together with the Physics Department at IFE, Kjeller organizer of the bi-annual Geilo PhD School. In 2018 the Geilo School has the title: "Evolving Soft Matter: Shape, Dynamics and Functionality", taking place March 11-21, 2019 at Bardøla Høyfjellshotel, Geilo, Norway. 4.5 ECTS.

MSc student projects

We search 3-5 Master student candidates who are motivated and wish to do curiosity driven projects or thesis experimental or theoretical research in our laboratory.

In some of the activities, there are possibilities for national or international collaborations, including possibilities for longer stays, for example in performing synchrotron X-ray and/or neutron experiments at international facilities such as ESRF-Grenoble-France, MaxIVLab-Lund-Sweden, LNLS-Campinas-Brazil, Diamond-Oxfordshire-UK, ILL-Grenoble-France, and various neutron research facilities wordlwide.

Our scientific collaborators for possible MSc projects, including their home laboratories are located in:

  • Norway (IRIS-Stavanger, IFE-Kjeller, University of Oslo)
  • France (Paris, Rennes)
  • Brazil (Rio de Janeiro, Sao Paulo, Recife, Brasilia, Campinas)
  • South-Korea (KAIST-Daejon, Gwangju)
  • Denmark (Copenhagen, DTU-Lyngby)
  • Cuba (Havana)
  • Finland (Aalto)
  • Portugal (Lisboa)
  • Germany (Bayreuth)
  • Czech Republic (Prague, Liberec)
  • The Netherlands (Amsterdam, Groeningen)
  • UK (Cambridge, Oxford, Manchester)
  • Sweden (Lund, Uppsala, Gothenburg)
  • USA (Harvard, MIT, Indiana, Florida, New Mexico, Texas)
  • Italy (Rome)
  • Japan (Tokyo)
