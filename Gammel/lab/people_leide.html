Leide Cavalcanti

Research Scientist PhD

ISIS Neutron Facility UK

Research activities:

Research scientist with experience in Synchrotron and Neutron facilities in (ESRF, LNLS, MaxLAB, PSI, MURR) using small angle X ray scattering, grazing incidence X ray diffraction on liquid surface, Langmuir monolayer, Brewster angle microscopy, cryo-TEM, fluorescence and light scattering to characterize liposomes for drug delivery, polymer-surfactant complexes and other materials. (http://www.researcherid.com/rid/B-1703-2013)
